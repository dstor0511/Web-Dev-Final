Your Artist Hands

By: Mapiz

Let me dance for you
let me fill your track with syncs for you
let me draw through a movement your steps
let me feel you in every flow of my body
let me dance with you while the wind surrounds us
let me know your favorite song so we can rehearse a duet
let me take your songs and play with them
let me create you the best of my compositions
let me take my scraps to make a new garment
let me set the track for you to bring my feet in sync with yours
Dancing for you is my greatest wish.
