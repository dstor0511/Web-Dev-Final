Big changes always start with an idea

A little big idea

Six friends, one mission, spread out God's love and hope using stickers and the internet.
A La Puerta was an invention of God. At one time in church, we were talking about a man who wrote the word eternity all over Sydney and became known for what he did. Who knows how many people he will touch with that simple word that implies so much. When I heard that, he was spinning in my head. I was on the bus like any other day, I had felt dissatisfied for a long time because I wasn't doing anything to talk to people about Jesus and maybe I could stop and talk to everyone on the bus, but talking is something that costs me a lot, so I was thinking of alternatives. God recorded in me the story of the man in Sydney and I thought we could do something similar. Perhaps not painting walls because in Colombia that's not well seen, but something that people see and makes them think of God. Then the idea of ​​stickers came, something cute and simple; it is a piece of paper but if it is aesthetically acceptable, many would keep it. I told my sister and my friends, and we started working on the project. The idea of ​​the website arose from the need to attract people to a church and give them tools in their life with God. Jesus wanted us to make disciples, that is, to form people to follow him and to form others. And that is the goal of wanting to show them the church as a family in God. Somehow, on the page, we are being family. Maybe we are not together, but we are helping each other. Finally, here we continue, we have not yet started distributing the stickers or helping people with the name of A La Puerta, but soon we will and we hope that God uses us for his purpose and to conquer Bogotá for Him.

Juanis - Founder of A La Puerta.
