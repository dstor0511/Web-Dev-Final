Your Artist Hands

By: Mapiz

You have my future in your hands
you are molding it,
sometimes you drop the clay
because I stumble many times,
sometimes your mix is different
Why do I doubt a lot?
but you keep molding it.
Your will is so perfect
your plans are so concrete,
you specify my life with a single touch,
that you even changed the colors
those that I already had in mind,
because my shade of purple It's not the same one that you wanted for me
you have my mold of the future in your hands,
you have them so dirty because you perfect it more and more
that sometimes I worry that it will be very perfect
but that's how you want it
I trust you
I trusted you
I will trust you
Why do I care if your mold, your clay, your colors are better than mine?
